Track your To-Dos

You're needed in all areas of your day-to-day. With Tasker, keep track of everything you may need to do on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis. At Tasker, we pride ourselves on the efficiency of our platform. Once you're done with a task, check it off of your To-Do list!

Prioritize what matters

Tasks have varying levels of importance. For these reasons, Tasker leverages state of the art technology to sort your logged tasks arithmetically. This way, you always know what needs your attention at any given moment.

Get your time back

With your daily tasks tracked and prioritized, you can go worry free. Getting your time and energy back so you can get back to what matters most.

About Us

Established in a garage in the suburbs outside of Chicago, Illinois, the idea to log tasks on an online app was thunk-ed up. The brain that turned its gears for this was none other than the founder and CEO Matthew Papesh. Ever since that faithful day in the Chicagoland fall of 2024, Matthew took his ideas to the Worcester Polytechnic Institute. With the support of interested investors, Tasker came to fruition to be your first stop for task-tracking.
